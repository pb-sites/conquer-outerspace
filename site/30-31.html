Crom shouted up to the towering giant, "Bellbrook, old buddy, I need you to catch me a fish. I'm looking for a big, ugly fish with scales as hard as diamonds."

The giant looked down at Crom and winked, "I know just the fish you need." Bellbrook grabbed his net and tumbled into the ocean, absolutely soaking everyone.
