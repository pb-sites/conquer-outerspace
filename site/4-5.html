The brothers were fearless! They ravaged make-believe towns like Viking Berserkers; sunk ships on the high seas like Cutthroat Pirates; blasted their way across the universe like Rogue Space Bandits; and robbed imaginary banks like Devilish Outlaws. The brothers had wild fin, but their games would always end with someone getting hurt, and that someone was always Omari, the younger brother.

Well see how fun it is living by yourself, smart guy. I'm done being your dumb little brother.
