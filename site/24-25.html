Eric could see clear across the planet for miles through Crom's specially designed, three-eyed telescope. He could see everything: his home planet, the swirling every-colored sands of strange deserts, gigantic crystals made of ice and snow in the north, and even his own crashed rocket ship; but Omari was nowhere to be seen.

Suddenly, Oats started barking wildly at a red cloud floating in the sky.

Eric tries to calm the dog down, "Oats, you dummy, that's just a cloud. Relax." But Eric was dead wrong. The red cloud was actually a flock of swirling, squawking-mad red birds, each bird the size of a slightly underweight hippopotamus. Without the slightest warning, the birds ATTACKED! One by one, the monsterous birds plucked Eric, Oats, and Crom off the mountaintop like three puny, squrming worms.
