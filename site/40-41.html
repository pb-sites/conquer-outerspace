Old men ran outside without their dentures; women in hair curlers ran around in their pajamas, and teenagers screamed like babies and hid under their beds. Everyone thought they were being invaded, and it was an invasion, an invasion of life into the dead, dull, and boring town of Stubbsville. The townspeople watched as out of the spaceship stepped strange monsters, wild beasts, and two kids everyone recognized immediately.

Eric and Omari stood before the crowd and started laughing. Eric turned to his brother and said, "Well Omari, we're home. Now what?"

Omari looked into the faces of all the people watching him and said, "Isn't it obvious? We pull these slugs out of the garbage dump and teach them how to make life on this planet exciting again."
