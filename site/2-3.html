Eric and Omari Thatcher were banned from just about every single house, playground, school, and backyard in Stubbsville. They were chased out of the library for playing Barbarian Invaders in the history section and banished from the town fountain for thrashing about wildly like writhing Sea Serpents. The brothers acted at all times like Horrible Monsters from Outer Space.
