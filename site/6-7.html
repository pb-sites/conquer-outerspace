After a terrible amount of name-calling and tons of grouchy face-making, Omari took off for the town junkyard and their dog, Oats followed. Oats absolutely hated jerks.

Eric sat in his empty tree fort alone. He tried being wild and reckless but he just felt sad and brother-less. Looking around he said, "What am I supposed to do now, make friends with the pinecones? All they do is hang around, and when they get bored of that they just drop to the ground and sit there in the dirt, even more bored than before!"

So Eric decided to hunt down Omari and make his brother's life miserable again! It didn't take long to hunt down Omari and make his brother's life miserable again! It didn't take Eric long to find Omari living inside an old junked-up rocket ship.
